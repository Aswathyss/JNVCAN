Back to the Hills 2024

School reunions are a time to celebrate friendships, new and old. They’re a reminder that we’re all in thi together, and that we’re never truly alone.


JNV Calicut Alumni Network is organizing the event with accommodations arranged for the overnight stay on Saturday December 28th.

This reunion is a special opportunity to reflect on our journey at JNV and celebrate the accomplishments of our alumni community. We have come a long way since we graduated, but the memories we made together will last a lifetime. Back to the Hills is a 2 Day long event that is planned for December 28 & 29th of this year.


“Hostel days were the best days of our life. Remember the endless laughter, the late night talks, and the shared meals. When we think back about those days, we can’t help but smile. we learned to live together, to share everything, and to support each other through thick and thin. And Back to the Hills 2024 gives you a chance to REPEAT that memory TOGETHER again.”
